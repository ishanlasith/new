Suggested Profile detail
volatility.exe -f dumpname.raw imageinfo

list all the process
volatility.exe -f dumpname.raw --profile=<profilename> pslist

list the history of Internet Explorer
volatility.exe -f dumpname.raw --profile=<profilename> iehistory

list the DLLs
volatility.exe -f dumpname.raw --profile=<profilename> dlllist

process listing in tree form
volatility.exe -f dumpname.raw --profile=<profilename> pstree

hidden processes
volatility.exe -f dumpname.raw --profile=<profilename> psscan

TCP/UDP connections 
volatility.exe -f dumpname.raw --profile=<profilename> sockets
